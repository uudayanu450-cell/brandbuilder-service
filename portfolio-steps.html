Digital Portfolio Showcase

Your resume tells them what you did; your portfolio shows them how you did it.

1. Setup Your Canvas

Create a free account on Notion and search for the "Personal Home" or "Portfolio" template.

2. Add "Proof of Work"

Don't just list projects. Add screenshots, links to live demos, and a brief description of the Problem, Solution, and Result.

3. Go Public

Click the "Share" button at the top right, toggle on "Share to web", and copy your link. This link goes on your LinkedIn and Resume!

1. Create Your Repo

Start a new repository named yourusername.github.io. This tells GitHub to host your files as a website.

2. Choose a Framework/Theme

You can push a simple index.html or use a Static Site Generator like Jekyll or Hugo for a blog-style portfolio.

Pro Tip: Use a clean README.md if you aren't ready for a full site yet.

3. Automate Your Brand

Link your repositories directly to your portfolio so recruiters can see your code commits in real-time.

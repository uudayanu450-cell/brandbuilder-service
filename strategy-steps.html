Personal Brand Strategy

Identify your strengths and build a unique professional story.

01. Identify Your Core Strengths

What makes you unique? List three technical skills and two soft skills.

02. Define Your Career Goals

Are you targeting corporate placements, startups, or freelance work? Your brand voice must match your target audience.

03. Build Your Professional Story

Create a "Pitch" that connects your past projects to your future ambitions.

"I am a [Major] student passionate about [Niche]. Having built [Project Name], I developed skills in [Skill], and I am now looking to apply this expertise to [Goal]."

04. Online Presence Strategy

Plan your engagement: What will you post about? Who will you network with?

  • Comment on 3 industry leaders' posts weekly.
  • Share one project update or "learning moment" every 10 days.
